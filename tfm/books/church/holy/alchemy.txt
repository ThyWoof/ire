  ..my wife wrapped up the said matter in wax, and I cut half
  an ounce of old lead and put it into a crucible in the fire,
  which being melted, my wife put in the said Medicine made
  up in a small pill or button, which presently made such a
  hissing and bubbling in its perfect operation, that within
  a quarter of an hour all the mass of lead was transmuted into
  the finest gold..
