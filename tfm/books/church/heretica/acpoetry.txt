                To Kill A Sukhoi

  [ This is a beautiful and tragic poem about the
   futility of war.

   In it, an F-15 shoots down an Su-25, but
   mortally wounded in the struggle, returns to
   his hangar to die with his master at his side,
   a common motif in aircraft literature.]
