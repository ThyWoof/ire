  I have it!  The diet be the key to the riddle!
  Grass-fed beeves give forth milk that turns blue and goes
  sour.

  Beeves fed upon mine other diet gave forth orange milk,
  that has lasted lo these many moons, yet remains fresh
  as the day it were milked!

  'Tis a pity the feed be so hard to obtain...
