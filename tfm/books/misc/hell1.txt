                The Tome of Elucidation

    Sinners, know that there is but one way to escape
    the lake of fire!

    Seek, therefore, the jug that forgives all sins,
    the Jug of Salvation, for verily it is known that
    any who drink from its sweet waters shall receive
    thereof, though he be in the pits of Hell itself.
